Lumbini Regional Hospital is located at the junction of Mahendra and Siddhartha highways in Butwal Municipality of Rupandehi District in the western part of Nepal which is Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha. Globally its position is 270 41′ 52″ (N) and 830 27’51” (E) longitude and 865 meters above sea level. It is situated at the foothills of Shivalik hills in Palpa and Rupandehi districts. This hospital covers some southwestern part of Syangja district of Gandaki zone, southern part of Parbat and Baglung district of Dhaulagiri zone, entire part of Pyuthan district of Rapti zone and half of Rukum Rolpa and Dang districts of Lumbini zone. Rapti area. The hospital covers a population of about four million (4 million) and thirteen districts in the western part of Nepal.
