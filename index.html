Welcome To Lumbini Provincial Hospital

This is Provincial Hospital is situated at the Junction of Mahendra and Sidhdharth highway in Butwal Sub

MetroMunicipality of Rupandehi District in the western part of Nepal near by is the birth place of Lord Buddha, lumbini. Globally its position is latitude 270 41′ 52″ (N) and longitude 830 27’51” (E) and 865 meter above sea level. It lies at the bottom of Siwalik hill of Palpa & Rupandehi district. This hospital covers all the districts of Lumbini zone including some south west part of Shyanja district of Gandaki zone, southern parts of Parbat and Baglung district of Dhaulagiri zone, whole part of Pyuthan district of Rapti zone and half parts of Rukum Rolpa and Dang district of Rapti zone. The hospital catches about 4 Million (40 Lakhs) population & thirteen districts of western part of Nepal. We also offer Anti recto viral therapy (ART), Voluntary Counseling and testing (VCT), Prevention Mother to Child Transmission (PMTCT) and Opportunistic Infection (OI) management services of HIV/AIDS Programs. Apart from curative, preventive, promotive and rehabilitative services, we provide Early Warning Reporting System (EWARS), in which Malaria, Kala-azar, Dengue Fever (DF)/Dengue Hemorrhagic Fever (DHF) / Dengue Shock Syndrome (DSS), Cholera, Severe Acute Respiratory illness (SARI), Fever, Enteric Fever, Leptospirosis, Hydrophobia and chicken Guniea related cases are watched and immediate report to respective department when they are record. We offer also Immunization Preventable disease (IPD) surveillance systems, in which Polio, Neonatal Tetanus, Encephalitis and Measles related cases watch and reports..
